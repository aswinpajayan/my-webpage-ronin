When mind has enough time to wander...

Topic needs no introduction. We are amidst one of the greatest adversities human kind have ever faced. And for the first time "that line felt like it is meant to be". We will overcome this. Scientists and researchers are toiling away endless days and nights pushing all boundaries, and they will find a vaccine. And we will bounce back as always. But when we do; Dont let this time be marked in your calendar with regrets.....

Cherish your time with your loved ones. We grew up and grew apart. But these grim times have pulled us together. For once we finally have time to sit and talk. About stars in the sky and everything underneath. Because now we know, we could have avoided that party and stayed for this chat.

Up skill

source

This image has been floating the internet recently. Amidst this situation allmost all technology based companies have allowed thier employees to work from home. Essential services are running on a lean workforce. And all colleges and universities have been closed down. This means most of us are spending quiet a huge amount of time glued to our smartphones/ computers. And we have plenty of time which in the recent past would have been lost in transit/partying etc. Make use of this time. Many online MOOC platforms have subsidised their courses. Universities and professors are hosting online MOOCS on their own. Take this time to upskill yourself. MIT open course ware is one among them.

Machine Learning and AI is one such field where there is tremendous interest. You can learn it from the pioneers or ivy league professors. The bookPattern Recognition and Machine Learning is used as introductory material to machine learning in leading universities around the world is made available online. There are lot of Beginner MOOC's like Andrew Ng's course on Coursera. SHALA is one such upcoming course by a group of Industry professionals and researchers from various organisations in India, including IITB and IISc.

Deep learning is one area of ML which is gaining immense popularity of the late. Mostly with availability of high performance GPUs and frameworks like Pytorch and Tensor Flow. GPUs in your local machine where a requirement to do deeplearnig for a while. But not anymore. Google Colab, Kaggle and similar websites allows you to use hosted runtimes to train your models. Meaning, now you can work on your deeplearning models from any device which have a good network connectivity. There are lot of introductory coures on Deep Learning. Most Universities, conduct short term crash courses as into to DL. One such noteworthy course done by MIT can be found here.

Geek up

Over time, even the most sophisticated and elegeant distro may look boring to you. we got all the time in the world. May be you should try out that one linux distribution you always wanted to try. Or that terminal emualtor which gives you cool new features on your terminal. Trying out these things will take hours of broswing and configuring. So usually we put them aside. But these tools when correctly configured can have huge impact on your efficieny.

For instance, I had been using neovim for almost two years now and is happy with it. Code completion works smoothly with ncm2 and jedi plugin. But things changed when I started using virtual env's. Auto completion broke and imported classes where not highlighted. Was tight on schedule and had to switch to vscode for the time being. But later realised that it was only minor problems with configurations. And there are some good semantic highlighting plugins availble.

While going through some blogs to fix this issue, I came across ranger. Ranger is a terminal based file browser with vim like key bindings. Ranger can do a lot of stuff. One cool feature is image display in terminal. But sadly I was using ubuntu and the default terminal emulator - the gnome-terminal. gnome-terminal cant render images in the terminal(or atleast it doesnt do it OOB). Next option was to use any other terminal emulator. After a little bit of googling, I came across 3 - urxvt, terminology and alacritty. After a little bit of tweaking and using a patched font, this is how my ranger in alacritty terminal looks like.

Connect with yourself

Take up that guitar. Sing some songs on smule. Dance to some random choreography in youtube. I have seen many friends put up their free hand sketches in insta. Some are doing calligraphy, yet others doing cooking. And ofcourse eveyone finished their favourite TV shows. Fitness groups are active with thier daily challenge routines. In short be creative, be productive, lure out negativity. Be thankfull to the health workers, researchers , police and other essential services. Cheer up, dont fall for fake news.
